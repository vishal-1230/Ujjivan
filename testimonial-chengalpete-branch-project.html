CHENGALPETE BRANCH PROJECT: (HANDING OVER DRINKING WATER TRACTOR TROLLEY TO MUNICIPALITY)

Ms. Mari Selvi Prabhu, Commissioner, Chengalpattu Municipality addressed the gathering on behalf of Chengalpet Municipality. She expressed her heartfelt gratitude to Ujjivan & Parinaam Foundation for donating the water tanker trolley to the municipality.

‘I have seen women carrying water up and down the hills to their homes. in the absence of any reliable suppy of drinking water. Our Municipality did have water tanker Lorries but they could not enter localities with narrow lanes. We thus were very enthusiastic about this project when the Ujjivan and Parinaam people first shared their proposal. The drnking water tractor trolley will make life easy for many people in the town’
