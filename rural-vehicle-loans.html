Vehicle Loans

Need to get around? Ab life mein pakado raftaar

Let it be for personal or business use, with our hassle-free Vehicle Loans, you can finally own your dream vehicle as you have always wanted to. Raftaar Loans gives customised financing options with maximum funding value to the on-road price of the vehicle.

Our loan approval and disbursal processes are quick, transparent, and require minimum documentation, making the whole loan application process simple and quick. Accelerating your dreams has now become easier now.

Two-Wheeler Loan

Wouldn’t life be dull without some swiftness? At Ujjivan Small Finance Bank, we help you materialise your dream of owning a two-wheeler, which promises to add comfort and convenience to your life. Owning a two-wheeler gives you the freedom to be where you need to be on time.

We offer up to ₹ 2.75 lakhs to buy the two-wheeler of your choice. Moreover, we endeavour to make the loan application process simple and straightforward requiring only basic documents and provide quicker decision time for loan sanction.

Avail our two-wheeler loans now to add speed and progress to your life!

Features and benefits

  • Customised financing options to cater to all types of 2-wheeler needs
  • Up to 95% funding on the on-road price
  • Flexible tenure options, ranging from 12 to 36 months
  • Hassle-free documentation
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Easy repayments

Eligibility

  • Age criteria: 18 to 59 years of age
  • Salaried/Self-employed with minimum work experience of one year

Minimum Documents Required

  • KYC documents
  • One security cheque
  • One passport size photograph

Schedule of Charges

Loan Amount ₹ 26,000 - ₹ 2,75,000
Loan Tenure 12 - 36 months.
Rate of Interest 21%- 23% p.a. (reducing balance).
Processing Fee 2% of loan amount (excluding applicable GST).
Documentation Charges ₹ 500 (excluding applicable GST).
Stamp duty As per the state by-laws.
CERSAI Charges At actuals
Late Payment Charges 2% flat on the overdue amount (excluding applicable GST).
Bounce Charges ₹ 450 per bounce.
Pre-closure Charges Pre-closure of loan will attract a pre-payment penalty of 2 % p.a. of principal outstanding (excluding applicable GST).

Three Wheeler Loan

Ujjivan Small Finance Bank provides holistic solutions to cater the financial requirements of Three Wheeler buyers. We have financing options available for both Diesel & Petrol Engine variants. The focus of these solutions is to provide timely credit with quick, hassle-free processing and simplified documentation.

Features and benefits

  • We provide customized financing options for both Diesel & Petrol Engine variants.
  • Up to 90% funding on Ex-Showroom price.
  • Flexible repayment options, ranging from 12 to 36 months.
  • Hassle-free documentation
  • Competitive interest rates

Eligibility

  • Individual between 21 to 59 years of age

Minimum Documents Required

  • Commercial Driving license.
  • One security cheque.
  • One passport size photograph.

Small Commercial Vehicle Loan

Ujjivan Small Finance Bank provides holistic solutions to cater to the financial requirements of aspiring Small Commercial Vehicle buyers. We have financing options available for all loading commercial vehicles weighing up to 3.5 tons. The focus of these solutions is to provide timely credit with quick, hassle-free processing, and simplified documentation.

Features & Benefits

  • Customized financing options for all types of commercial vehicle with weighing capacity of up to 3.5 tons
  • Up to 90% funding on ex-showroom price
  • Flexible repayment options, ranging from 12 to 60 months.
  • Hassle free documentation.
  • Competitive Interest Rates.

Eligibility

  • Individual between 21 to 59 years of age.

Minimum Documents Required

  • KYC Documents
  • One security cheque
  • One passport size photograph
  • Proof of house ownership (Based on assessment or scheme)

Schedule of Charges

Loan Amount ₹ 50,000 - ₹ 10,00,000
Loan Tenure 12 - 60 months(Maximum tenure is dependent on segment of the applicant).
Rate of Interest 15% - 17% p.a. (reducing balance).
Processing Fee 1% of loan amount (excluding applicable GST)
Documentation Charges ₹ 1000 (excluding applicable GST)
Stamp duty As per the state by-laws.
CERSAI Charges At actuals.
Late Payment Charges 2% PM of the overdue amount Plus GST
Bounce Charges ₹ 450 per bounce.
Pre-closure Charges Pre-closure of loan will attract a pre-payment penalty of 3% p.a. of principal outstanding (excluding applicable GST)
