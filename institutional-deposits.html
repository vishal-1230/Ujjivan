TASC FIXED DEPOSITS

Earn an attractive interest rate with our TASC Fixed Deposits. We offer flexibility in our deposits that enables you in making short and long-term investment decisions.

 

Features:

  • Minimum amount Rs. 25,000 and in multiples of Rs. 1,000 thereof
  • A minimum period of 7 days
  • A maximum period of 10 years
  • Interest payment options - monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, annually and at maturity.
  • Premature closure and partial withdrawal allowed
  • The rate of interest on FDs varies with tenure and amount
