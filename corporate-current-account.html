CURRENT ACCOUNT

Benefits of Ujjivan Current Account:

  • Flexible Free Cash Deposit Limit across branches (based on average balance of previous or current month as applicable).
  • Enjoy Cash & Cheque pickup facility at your Doorstep (Offered in selected cities through third party tie-ups)
  • No additional charges for non-home branch transactions - Every branch is home branch!!
  • Unlimited free ATM transactions at all Ujjivan Small Finance Bank ATMs
  • Financial transactions through Branch, ATM, Net Banking and Mobile Banking
  • Point of Sales Solutions (POS) - Stand-alone POS terminals / Wireless POS Terminals – GP terminals (Offered through third party tie ups)
  • Enjoy the facilities of SMS banking and Missed call banking along with 24/7 support from toll free phone banking center
  • Remittance facilities - NEFT/RTGS/IMPS

TASC Current Account

Carry your business transactions effectively

We bring to you a TASC – Trust, Association, Society and Club – Current Account that enables non-profits to carry business transactions effectively. A TASC organisation has special responsibilities and requirements for carrying out business operations smoothly. We assure a seamless banking experience to all non-profits, which addresses all their needs.

Features:

  • No average monthly balance criteria
  • No initial deposit for account opening
  • A monthly hard copy of the statement provided on request, free of cost (unless the customer has opted for e-statement)
  • MAB Based free electronic fund transfers per month
  • Free cash deposits across all Ujjivan Small Finance Bank branches up to 15 times of the monthly average balance (MAB) maintained during previous month

Benefits

  • Unlimited cash withdrawal at the branch
  • Doorstep banking
  • Monthly e-statement
  • Transaction alerts and updates of your account
  • Payable at par cheque book with 50 leaves free of cost per month
  • 24x7 customer care
  • Non-cash services (DD transactions at a nominal cost)

Value Added Services

  • CMS
  • POS
  • NACH (National Automated Clearing House)
  • Internet Payment Gateway

Business Edge Current Account

Cutting-edge advantage to your business

Our Business Edge Current Account offers comprehensive banking solutions, exclusively designed to help retailers as well as Micro and Small enterprises (MSEs) customers carry out business as per their changing needs

It has all the features of a Current Account with a dash of other value-added services which sets it in a class apart. Since small businesses and start-ups are flexible about their deposits and balances, we cater to different types of businesses with diverse needs.

Features:

  • 1) Decide your own cash deposit limit according to your business seasonality –we introduce Flexi Next and Flexi Now, to give your business the flexibility it needs.
    Flexi Next (based on following slabs of Monthly Average Balance or MAB)
    • MAB: < ₹1 Lakh: 12 times of average balance maintained in the PREVIOUS month or ₹5 lakhs, whichever is higher.
    • MAB: ₹1 Lakh - < ₹5 Lakhs: 15 times of average balance maintained in the PREVIOUS month.
    • MAB: >= ₹5 Lakhs: 20 times of average balance maintained in the PREVIOUS month.
    Flexi Now (based on following slabs of Monthly Average Balance or MAB)
    • MAB: < ₹1 Lakh: 12 times of average balance maintained in the CURRENT month or ₹5 lakhs whichever is higher.
    • MAB: ₹1 Lakh - < ₹5 Lakhs: 15 times of average balance maintained in the CURRENT month
    • MAB: >= ₹5 Lakhs: 20 times of average balance maintained in the CURRENT month
  • 2) Free RTGS and NEFT services through any channel
  • 3) Waiver on POS and CMS charges on the maintenance of required balance criteria
  • 4) No additional charges for non-home branch transactions
  • 5) Enjoy the benefits of RuPay Platinum Debit Card
  • 6) Enhanced transaction limits of ₹1,50,000 per day at ATMs and shopping
  • 7) 2 domestic airport lounge access free per quarter across 30+ lounges pan-India
  • 9) Unlimited free ATM transactions at all Ujjivan ATMs and 10 free transactions at other bank ATMs per month
  • 10) 50 free cash withdrawals at Ujjivan branches every month
  • 11) 150 free cheque leaves per quarter
  • 12) MAB can be as low as ₹25,000 Only

Rewards and benefits: For every incremental balance maintained in the account, enjoy the benefits that provide an edge to your business

Note: The features and benefits on RuPay Platinum Debit Card are provided by RuPay and can be discontinued at any time without any prior notice to the customer at the sole discretion of the Bank/RuPay.

Eligibility:

To be eligible for a Business Edge Current Account, you must have any of the following:

  • Resident individual
  • Sole proprietorship
  • Partnership firm
  • Limited liability partnership
  • Limited company
  • One Person Company
  • Hindu undivided family
  • Section 8 / Section 25 Companies
  • Trust, Association, Society, Club

Other details:

  • Need for maintenance of monthly average balance of ₹25,000 per month
  • Non-maintenance of the minimum balance entails a fee of ₹750 + GST per month.

In keeping with the overall bank tradition, the Premium Current Account provides you with additional benefits and privileges to facilitate your business’ seamless functioning and smooth operations.

Key features:

  • Enjoy the benefits of RuPay Classic debit card with unlimited free ATM transactions at Ujjivan SFB ATMs and 6 free transactions at other bank ATMs per month
  • Enhanced limits of ₹1,00,000 per day in ATM withdrawals and shopping
  • Free e-statement
  • 50 free cash withdrawals at Ujjivan SFB branches every month
  • Transaction alerts informing of updates on your account
  • 24x7 customer care (available in your preferred language)
  • Non-cash services (DD and PO transactions at a nominal cost)

Eligibility:

To be eligible for a Premium Current Account, you must have any of the following:

  • Resident individual
  • Sole proprietorship
  • Partnership firm
  • Limited liability partnership
  • Limited company
  • One Person Company
  • Hindu undivided family
  • Section 8 / Section 25 Companies
  • Trust, Association, Society, Club

Other details:

  • Need for maintenance of MAB of ₹10,000 per month
  • Non-maintenance of the minimum balance entails a fee of ₹500 + GST per month.

For the ease of doing business

For a growing business, you need to transact briskly through multiple channels. Our Regular Current Account is designed to address all your business banking needs, while providing ease in carrying out transactions. You can stay updated and transact from anywhere, at any time and concentrate on growing your business and customers
