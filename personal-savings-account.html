Privilege Savings Account

Something special, for those who are special to us

Ujjivan SFB’s Privilege Savings Account comes with a host of benefits and privileges that gives you more value for money.

It is easy to open this account. Just walk-in to the nearest Ujjivan SFB branch or call home our representative for easy doorstep account opening.

Eligibility:

To be eligible for Privilege Savings Account, individual needs to meet any one of the below criteria:

  • Monthly Average Balance in the Savings Account – Rs.25,000 & above
  • Cumulative FD – Rs.5,00,000 & above
  • Single RD >= Rs.10,000 p.m. for minimum tenure of 12 months
  • Salary credit of Rs.30,000 and above per month*

*(Only one criterion applicable for Privilege Corporate Salary Account)

Features

Minor Savings Account

For the apple of your eye

Teach your children the basics of banking and make sure to give them a comfortable and secure future. Start investing and saving early for your children and empower your children financially.

Our Minor Savings Account is the perfect start to their banking journey. This account can be opened for children in the age group of 1 day to 18 years on the availability of necessary documents. You can start an account for your child, which is held jointly by you. With access to savings through a Debit Card (on request for children above 10 years only), you can guide your child’s transactions until the age of 18.

Enjoy the flexibility to use the savings without worrying about maintaining a minimum balance. We believe that a financially literate young generation goes on to achieve financial empowerment. We applaud your choice to encourage your children to start saving early!

Features:

  • No average monthly balance criteria.
  • No charges on non-maintenance of minimum balance
  • An account can be opened jointly with the guardian only
  • Operated by the parent/guardian until the minor becomes a major
  • Can be converted into a regular savings account when your child turns into a major (18 Years)
  • Provision of Debit card for minors above 10 years (upon request)

We provide convenient digital channels exclusively for you to open a Savings Account from the comfort of your home. You can now open your digital account within minutes by following a few simple steps. Just remember to keep your Aadhaar card and PAN card handy for instant account opening.

Open a Digital Savings Account today, to start banking with us immediately without any hassle of paper-work.
