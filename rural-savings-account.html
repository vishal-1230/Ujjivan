It is easy to open this account. Just walk-in to the nearest Ujjivan SFB branch or call home our representative for easy doorstep account opening.

Eligibility:

To be eligible for Privilege Savings Account, individual needs to meet any one of the below criteria:

  • Monthly Average Balance in the Savings Account – Rs.25,000 & above

Minor Savings Account

For the apple of your eye

Teach your children the basics of banking and make sure to give them a comfortable and secure future. Start investing and saving early for your children and empower your children financially.

Our Minor Savings Account is the perfect start to their banking journey. This account can be opened for children in the age group of 1 day to 18 years on the availability of necessary documents. You can start an account for your child, which is held jointly by you. With access to savings through Personal Net Banking and a Debit Card (on request for children above 10 years only), you can guide your child’s transactions until the age of 18.

Enjoy the flexibility to use the savings without worrying about maintaining a minimum balance. We believe that a financially literate young generation goes on to achieve financial empowerment. We applaud your choice to encourage your children to start saving early!

Features:

  • No average monthly balance criteria.
  • No charges on non-maintenance of minimum balance
  • An account can be opened jointly with the guardian only
  • Operated by the parent/guardian until the minor becomes a major
  • Can be converted into a regular savings account when your child turns into a major (18 Years)
  • Provision of Debit card for minors above 10 years (upon request)

Transaction facilities:

  • Free SMS alert facility to your registered mobile number
  • Free quarterly statement on your registered email ID
  • NEFT, RTGS and IMPS available, standard charges apply

Channel Access:

  • 24*7 Unlimited transactions at Ujjivan SFB’s ATM

Digital Savings Account

We provide convenient digital channels exclusively for you to open a Savings Account from the comfort of your home in a few simple steps. You can open your Digital Account in just a few minutes. Just keep your Aadhaar card and PAN card handy for Instant Account opening.

You deserve quicker and better solutions to match your fast-paced lifestyle. Open Digital Saving account with us – save time while saving your money. Start transacting from your digital savings account as soon as the online account opening is complete. Enjoy seamless fund transfers through our Mobile Baking/Internet Banking platforms.

Start banking with us without any hassle of paper-work.

Features of Digital Savings Account:

  • Open a Savings Account instantly
  • Zero balance account. No need to worry about minimum balance.
  • Free debit card for the first year
  • Free SMS alert facility to your registered mobile number
